About Quantum Dispatch

Quantum Dispatch is a Star Citizen podcast dedicated to exploring the ever-expanding universe of Star Citizen. Our mission is to bring you the latest news, in-depth discussions, and unique perspectives from passionate players and community members. Whether you're a seasoned pilot, a lore enthusiast, or just starting your journey in the 'verse, Quantum Dispatch is your go-to source for all things Star Citizen.

Each episode, we dive into the latest updates, share our adventures, discuss ships and mechanics, and highlight the vibrant community that makes Star Citizen special. We believe in fostering a welcoming and informative space for all Citizens, and we love connecting with our listeners through stories, interviews, and community spotlights.

Join us as we navigate the stars, uncover hidden gems, and celebrate the creativity and camaraderie that define the Star Citizen experience. Thank you for tuning in and being part of our journey!
